Course Selection

For CSIE students, every year our department office will arrange course schedules for us. You can either follow the schedule they make or arrange your own schedule, it is all up to you. Every semester, there will be 3 course selection periods. The first one is the end of the last semester (1 semester before), for new students, it will be around 1 month before their semester starts (during the break). Second period is the first 2 weeks of a new semester. In this period students get to sit in a class and decide whether they want to take the course or not. You can either add or drop classes in this period. Third period is the 3rd week of a new semester. To add a course in this period, you have to ask for a QR code from the teacher. And once you scan the barcode, you cannot drop it anymore.

Before every course selection period, you can always arrange your course schedule in advance (after filling out the half-term teaching feedback --> only for old students). Note that you can only select courses during one of the 3 periods mentioned above

After midterm, you can choose to drop only one class or none at all. All you have to do is to ask the teacher (or the teaching assistant) for a QR code, scan it, and input your information.
