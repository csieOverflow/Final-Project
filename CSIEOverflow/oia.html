OIA

OIA (Office of International Affairs) is the office which is responsible for the promotion of international services in the university, to provide high efficiency and a friendly environment with the goal of creating a superior learning environment for international students. They deal with the administration of international students before and after they reach Taiwan, providing student volunteers for new students, etc.
