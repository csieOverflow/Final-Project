Graduation Requirements

Each department requires different amount of credits in order to graduate.There are different credit requirements every year, some years will require more and some will require less. But no worries! By the time you are approaching graduation, you will be able to choose which study year's requirements you are following (you can choose any year you want).

You can click here to check the total number of credits you have already done. (Including the credit categories and total credits in each one)

General Education Requirements

You will have to finish 9 credits of Language subjects, 4 credits of Physical Education, 2 credits of Service Learning, 2 credits of Information Technology, 20 credits of General Education Elective Cores, English Language Proficiency exam (you would have to do an extra of 4~6 english course credits if you don't want to take the test), and Physical Fitness Test.

Please refer to this link for the full list of NDHU General Education requirements.


Departmental Requirements

In addition to the General Education Requirements, CSIE students are required to take 3 major programs and at least one of two Professional Elective programs from the Department of CSIE, or any program-set from any department (deemed as a minor), and study a minimum of 132 total credits in order to graduate.

   1. Fundamental Program of Electrical Engineering and Computer Science : 23 credits
   2. Core Program of Computer Science(I) : 23 credits
   3. Core Program of Computer Science (II) : 23 credits
   4. One Professional Elective Program (alternative):
      -Program of Multimedia and Intelligent Computing：21 credits
      -Program of Network and System：21 credits
      or any program-set (core or elective) with at least 21 credits from other departments.

For more detailed important rules and regulations, please refer to our CSIE NDHU website.
