Autonomous Hours

Every NDHU student has to finish 65hours of autonomous learning to be able to graduate. The total hours might be different depending on the year you are admitted. The total hours are divided into 3 categories, which are: Service Dedication, Multi-Progress and Professional Development.

Service Dedication

There are a lot of things that you can do to fill-out our Service Dedication hours. You can either go to the department office and ask for some work to do (running errands), or you can also be a volunteer at various school events.

Multi-Progress

Multi-Progress hours are the easiest ones to get. You can attend the events which are held in school such as workshops, move nights or even concerts.

Professional Development

For Professional Development, there is not much you can attend. The events whose hours are counted in this section are only those which are held by your own department. For us(CSIE), the department will hold computer related seminars and workshops. You have to attend these in order to get your Professional Development hours filled.

TIPS :

"thing you can do to get Service Dedication or Multi-Progress hours is to organize events with your classmates and apply for hours from the autonomous learning website. Just take as many pictures as you can as a proof, apply for reasonable amount of hours for the activity (max. 6hrs) and submit it together with your classmates. The school will decide whether or not you can get the hours."
