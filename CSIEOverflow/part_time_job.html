How to Find a Part Time Job

Types of Jobs

A part-time job can be a great option for students who want to make extra cash or take a break from their loaded coursework. While college students can be offered grants and loans, they're not always offered enough financial aid to cover full tuition. A part-time job allows them to work toward paying off their higher-level education. Here are types of jobs you may consider:

1) Server
2) English Teacher
3) Teaching Assistant
4) Babysitter
5) Cleaner

Documents

We have to apply for our Work Permit online in order to get one. Meanwhile, the docs we would need to apply for it are listed below:
1) ARC card
2) Student Status Certificate
3) Passport
4) Student ID card

How to Apply

In order to apply for the job, you can either go directly to the place where you wish to work at and ask if there is any vacancy, or look for one online. You might also go to this facebook page and see if there is any places hiring at current time.
