Administration

Tuition fee

Tuition fee varies depending on which department you are in. Taiwanese and International Students generally pay different amount of tuition fee. Even though the tuition fees for international students is higher, we are eligible to apply for the NDHU scholarship (Taiwanese students cannot), which is at least 50% off (will be explained below in the scholarship section).

Scholarship

There are a lot of scholarship types for international students in Taiwan (MOFA, MOE, ICDF, etc). A lot of NDHU international students will apply for the NDHU scholarship (exclusive for NDHU International students who do not have any other kind of scholarship). For MOFA, MOE or ICDF, students would have to apply through the embassy and can only do so before they come to Taiwan. NDHU scholarship is the most popular scholarship among international students, thus, it is the most competitive one. There are 3 types of NDHU scholarship which are:

1. Type A - 100% tuition waiver + stipend

2. Type B - 100% tuition waiver

3. Type C - 50% tuition waiver

If you apply for this scholarship, you would most probably get a type-C scholarship which is 50% tuition waiver. To be eligible to apply for this scholarship, you have to have a GPA above 2.0

Taiwan Bank

Taiwan bank is the site where we can get and print out our payment information, such as dormitory fee if you live in the dormitory, tuition fee, and any other miscellaneous fees.

Installment

We can always apply for an installment every semester. If you are in CSIE department, the department office will send the newest installment form for you to fill out in the beginning of every semester. All you have to do is to fill out the application form, sign on it, pass it to the Department office to validate, and pay the first installment in the Administration building. Don't forget that you would have to attach your payment sheet from Taiwan Bank together with the application form before you bring it to the dept. office.
